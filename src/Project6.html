Project Overview

Meal Hero is the ultimate app designed to simplify grocery shopping and meal planning for students. With Meal Hero, users can effortlessly search for recipes, plan meals within a budget, and set notifications and reminders for upcoming meals and grocery runs. The app also features group forums where students can share tips, recipes, and meal prep hacks, fostering a supportive community environment as well as a video feed to scroll through and watch videos to save and later follow cooking instructions,. With the goal of making meal prepping and planning a breeze, Meal Hero empowers students to eat healthily, save money, and make the most out of their college experience.

Design brief

The design brief for the Meal Hero app began with a research goal focused on enhancing the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of grocery shopping. Through a rigorous interview process, employing a strict recruitment screener, we uncovered common findings: participants faced time constraints, desired meal variety, experienced food waste, and sought meal inspiration from various online platforms. Leveraging these insights, we developed an app catering to these needs, aiming to streamline grocery shopping and meal planning for users, ultimately making the process more convenient and enjoyable.
