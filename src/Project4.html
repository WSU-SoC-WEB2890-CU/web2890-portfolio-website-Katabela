Project Overview

The Wonderment by Kidnected World provides spaces for kids to take action in their local and global communities. Kids, schools and organizations come together to create a world of good, It’s a platform where kids can meet the world and others, share and collaborate creatively On a global level, and even make tangible impact in the world, all in a safe space designed for them.

Design brief

The design brief for this non-profit organization app began with the development of the app idea, creation of personas, and extensive user research to understand user needs and behaviors. Through iterative rounds of low-fidelity prototypes and rigorous user testing, we addressed functionality issues, content discrepancies, and other usability concerns to perfect the app’s design. The iterative design process prioritized user feedback, ensuring that the final product meets the needs of the target audience while effectively serving the mission of The Wonderment.
