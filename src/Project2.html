User Experience Design & Prototype Project

Project Overview

CaptionIt is a dynamic web platform designed to revolutionize the way social media users engage with their audience through captivating captions. Tailored specifically for individuals aged 16 to 30, CaptionIt offers an intuitive interface featuring a caption generator where users can effortlessly upload photos and select from a variety of predefined styles to create impactful captions. With curated caption categories, real-life examples, and a wealth of expert tips in its blog section, CaptionIt helps users bypass the time-consuming task of coming up with witty captions themselves.

Design brief

The design process for CaptionIt was driven by thorough user research and iterative testing to ensure the final product effectively met the needs of its target audience. Initial research, including surveys, interviews, and pilot testing provided insights into social media habits and caption creation processes, informing the development of detailed personas guiding the design process. Identified issues, such as the time-consuming nature of caption creation, were addressed through brainstorming sessions and concept exploration. Low-fidelity and medium-fidelity prototypes facilitated feedback on user interactions. High-fidelity prototypes closely resembled the final product and underwent comprehensive testing to identify pain points and areas for improvement. Feedback from testing sessions was incorporated into the final design, to strive for an easy to use platform that prioritizes the needs and preferences of its target audience!
